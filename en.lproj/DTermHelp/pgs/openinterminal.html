Running commands in Terminal

Some commands may require a full interactive terminal to function, or you may wish to keep a persistent window open for a series of commands. In these cases, DTerm can open a new window in the system’s Terminal application, set it to the correct directory, and execute the command you have entered. Simply choose “Execute in Terminal” from the action menu, or press Command-Return instead of just Return.
